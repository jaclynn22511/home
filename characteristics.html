Appearance/Description

		
		
The dodo was an omnivorous bird that had feathers similar to those of pigeons, as well as a hooked beak. Information about its appearance cannot be fully accurate, because it is extinct. The “Ice Age” movie (from 2002) portrays them as plump and purple birds.
